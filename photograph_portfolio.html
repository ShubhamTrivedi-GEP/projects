About Me

I'm Emma Wilson, a professional photographer with over 10 years of experience capturing the beauty of our world. My work spans various genres including landscape, portrait, architectural, and travel photography.

I believe that photography is not just about taking pictures but about telling stories and preserving emotions. Every click of my camera is an attempt to freeze a moment in time that might otherwise be forgotten.

My work has been featured in National Geographic, Vogue, and various international exhibitions. I'm based in San Francisco but available for projects worldwide.
